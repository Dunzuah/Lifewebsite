Our Guiding Light

Mission

Promote sustainable development, human rights, anti-corruption, equal access to quality education, and justice for all in Liberia — especially vulnerable groups and girls.

Vision

A fair, inclusive Liberia where transparent institutions drive prosperity, human dignity is inviolable, and every child accesses excellent education.

Core Values

Integrity Accountability Equity Volunteerism Transparency Innovation
